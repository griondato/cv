Greta Riondato

Artist, Web Designer and Developer

Greta Riondato is an Italian painter, sculptor, designer and mother of two, who lives and works among the thriving arts community of Guelph, Ontario. Born and raised in the Veneto region of North Italy, Greta has been immersed in the world of art her entire life. She was enrolled in an artistic high school, and later completed a degree in fashion design at I.U.A.V. Venice. Working from photographs, she creates stunningly realistic and undeniably unique oil-based portraits with a well-studied, delicately distinctive style.
